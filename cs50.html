About My CS50 Journey

Welcome to my CS50 Learning Journey. CS50, Harvard University's introduction to computer science and the art of programming, has been a great experience. This site documents my progress, projects, and the resources that have helped me along the way.

Progress

I undertook the CS50 course to deeply understand computer science fundamentals. Here's a snapshot of my progress:

  • Completed Weeks 1 to 10
  • Learned C, Python, and JavaScript
  • Developed several projects and problem sets
  • Engaged with the CS50 community through forums

Projects & Assignments

  • Project 1: Hello

    A simple program that prints "Hello, World!" in multiple languages.

  • Project 2: Mario

    Recreating the classic Mario pyramid using loops and conditional statements.

  • Project 3: Caesar Cipher

    Implementing a Caesar cipher for basic encryption and decryption.

  • Project 4: Web Programming

    Developed a dynamic website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
